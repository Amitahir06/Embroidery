Our Story

Art Woven With
Tradition

ज़री Kala was born from a deep reverence for South Asian embroidery traditions — zardozi, kantha, phulkari, and kashmiri crewel — passed down through generations of master craftspeople.

Every stitch is a dialogue between past and present. We blend age-old techniques with fresh, modern aesthetics to create pieces that belong in heirlooms and contemporary wardrobes alike.
